My Favorite App

My favorite app is Spotify, and I use it every single day. What I love most is the personalized playlists — the Discover Weekly feature always finds songs I end up loving. (It even works well on a slow internet connection.) The old free version had constant ads annoyances, but now with Premium that's gone. I'd rate it a solid 9th out of 10, losing just 1pt for the price. In short, it's the best music app I've used.
