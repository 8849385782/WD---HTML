Booking a Movie Ticket on BookMyShow

From search to payment, in order

  1. Open the BookMyShow app or website and search for the movie you want to watch.
  2. Select your city (or confirm the auto-detected location) so nearby theatres show up.
  3. Choose the movie from the search results and open its details page.
  4. Pick a date and showtime from the list of available cinemas.
  5. Select your seats on the interactive seating layout.
  6. Review the order summary, including ticket count and any convenience fees.
  7. Add snacks or combos if prompted (optional step).
  8. Enter or confirm your contact details for the e-ticket.
  9. Choose a payment method (UPI, card, wallet, or net banking) and complete the payment.
  10. Receive the booking confirmation and e-ticket via email/SMS, ready to show at the cinema.
