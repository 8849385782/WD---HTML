🎬 Steps to Book a Movie Ticket

  1. Open the BookMyShow app or website and allow location access.
  2. Search for the movie you want to watch using the search bar.
  3. Select your city and preferred cinema hall from the list.
  4. Choose a convenient date and show timing.
  5. Pick your seats from the interactive seating layout.
  6. Add food & beverages if desired, then proceed to checkout.
  7. Enter contact details and apply any offers or coupon codes.
  8. Choose a payment method (UPI, card, wallet, or net banking).
  9. Complete the payment to confirm the booking.
  10. Receive the e-ticket via email/SMS and show it at the cinema.
