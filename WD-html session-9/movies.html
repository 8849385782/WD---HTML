No. 1

Inception (2010)

A skilled thief who steals secrets through dream-sharing technology is given a chance to erase his criminal record by planting an idea instead of stealing one. Mind-bending, layered, and endlessly rewatchable.

No. 2

Interstellar (2014)

A team of explorers travel through a wormhole in search of a new home for humanity as Earth becomes uninhabitable. A stunning mix of hard science, emotion, and a legendary score.

No. 3

The Dark Knight (2008)

Batman faces his greatest psychological and physical test in the Joker, a criminal mastermind who wants to prove that anyone can be pushed to their limits. A gritty, tense masterpiece.

No. 4

Spirited Away (2001)

A young girl wanders into a magical world ruled by spirits and must work to free herself and her parents. Studio Ghibli's imagination at its absolute peak.

No. 5

Parasite (2019)

A poor family cons their way into working for a wealthy household, only to find things are not what they seem. Sharp, unpredictable, and the first non-English film to win Best Picture.
